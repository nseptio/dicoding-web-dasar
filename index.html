Kirifuji Nagisa

Profile

Introduction

One of the three presidents of the Tea Party, Trinity's student council, and the leader of the Filius Faction. Nagisa is also Mika's childhood friend. In contrast to Mika, Nagisa thinks first before acting, sometimes thinking a bit too much to the point where she causes problems for others who are uninvolved or are supposed to be considered innocent. Compared to the other Tea Party members, Nagisa's political prowess seems to be the highest. She enjoys preparing tea leaves and sweets for the Tea Party, and many students are dreaming of the day when they'll be invited to the Tea Party, which is ironic since the Tea Party was supposed to revolve around social gatherings, yet Nagisa never invited many people.

Personality

Nagisa is quite calm and composed, but like everyone else, she has her limit, and she can get quite ruthless once someone breaks her composure. She also tends to overthink things which can sometimes end up causing trouble for other people. Even Sensei called her cynical due to her paranoic behavior.

Appearance

Nagisa has long ash blonde hair with flower ornaments stretching from the top of her head to her left ear. On her back are a pair of angel wings that sprouts out from the waist.

Halo

Her halo consists of a red circle, that has ten thorns sticking out of it. Every other thorn is thinner, and has a semi-circle base. They also merge with the circle, unlike the rest.

Uniform

She wears a mid-length, cream skirt dress, with golden buttons, and accents around the edges of the skirt. The inner side of the skirt is navy. On top of this, she wears her Tea Party capelet with similar design, tied with a dark grey bow. Below her bosom, she has a belt, which holds a pistol holster. For legwear, she has a pair of dark grey pantyhose, and white high heels.

Firearm

She uses a white Walther PPK pistol with a pink lower slide and grip. The Trinity insignia is imprinted on the grip. Her name is stamped on the front slide. For her EX Skill, she commands a battery of L118 Light Guns.

Relationship

Tea Party

Misono Mika

A childhood friend who has known each other for around 10 years. Mika is also a president of the Tea Party alongside her. Although they both are quite the opposites when it comes to their personalities, they still kept their bond as friends, even after the events that happened in Volume 3.

Yurizono Seia

They are both presidents of the Tea Party.

Supplementary Lessons Department

Ajitani Hifumi

Nagisa seems to have a liking towards Hifumi, although her exact reasons are still unclear. Even though Nagisa was forced to put Hifumi in the Supplementary Lessons Department and risk getting her expelled from Trinity to confirm her suspicions, she still showed major concern and sympathy towards Hifumi.

Shirasu Azusa

During V3C2E15, Azusa knocked Nagisa out inside her safehouse under Hanako's orders by releasing a whole magazine worth of bullets onto her to keep her quiet.

Urawa Hanako

During V3C2, Hanako played a big role in keeping Arius away as it was mostly thanks to the plan she devised that Arius was able to be stopped. Part of this plan is keeping Nagisa quiet, which is exactly what she ordered Azusa to do. Right before Nagisa was knocked out, Hanako teased her a bit by saying that Hifumi is no longer friends with her.

Supplementary Lessons Department

Utazumi Sakurako

During V3C4E2, Sakurako uninvitedly joined a personal meeting held by Nagisa specifically for Sensei to talk about what happened after the Arius incident, causing the atmosphere to turn awkward.

Knights Hospitaller

Aomori Mine

During V3C4E2, Mine also joined Nagisa's personal meeting held specifically for Sensei regarding the aftermath of the Arius incident, disrupting the previously normal atmosphere.

Schale

Sensei

During Volume 3, Nagisa asked Sensei to help with guiding the Supplementary Lessons Department. Eventually, Sensei found out about Nagisa's intentions and decided to help them their own way, causing Nagisa to alter multiple factors that would make Sensei's job much more difficult.

Trivia

  • Nagisa is the second student to get a design change after her original design was already released. The first one is Kazusa.
    • Her design change was given during Volume F's release.
  • Coincidentally, Nagisa shares the same birthday as America's Independence Day although she was supposed to resemble Britain and its traditions, hence the tea and sweets
  • Her JP banner uses an English title instead of the usual JP titles: "Where there's Tea Party, there's hope."
  • Kiri (桐) and Fuji (藤) flowers are both associated with images of nobility in Japanese tradition, like Kiritsubo and Fujitsubo in The Tale of Genji.
